Information

EXPLORE also known as FBLA is a dungeon crawler and rouge-lite made by HunterSlimeG (Samuel) and Proppernubs (Matthew). It was made orginally for the Computer Game and Simulation Programming FBLA event. The game features a Indiana Jones inspired character and premise. The game's goal is to achieve the most amount of points, and successfully escape, while traveling around the procedurally generated map.
